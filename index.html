OUR DEPARTMENTS

CARDIOLOGY

Ohio Hospital & Medical Centre has a team of highly experienced cardiologists. Our Cardiology section is equipped with fully integrated cardiovascular unit with all aspects of cardiovascular care under one roof. At Ohio an ideal infrastructure has been built for cardiac patients. Our Non Invasive cardiac division is equipped with Colour Doppler Echo Machine, TMT, Holter Monitoring and Spirometry.

GYNAECOLOGY

Starting from special needs of would be or new mothers to the general gynaecological issues, at Ohio we deal everything with care. We make it certain that our Gynaecological and Obstetric team provide the patients with not just treatment but experience.

ORTHOPAEDIC

Our specialists at Ortho Department are well trained and experienced to treat any type of orthopaedic ailments. Advanced techniques and early rehabilitation are very significant in orthopaedic treatment. At Ohio, we look after the physical and mental well being of our patients after any orthopaedic surgery.

PHYSIOTHERAPY

At Ohio Hospital & Medical Centre, the Physiotherapy Department is equipped with the latest technology to handle physiotherapy needs. The friendly and well trained staffs are qualified enough to handle this. They undergo regular Professional Development Courses to update themselves.
